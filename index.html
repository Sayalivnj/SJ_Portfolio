About Me.

I design to make everyday life a little simpler and a lot more meaningful. With experience in UI/UX, system design and Human-Machine Interaction, I approach every project like a puzzle from understanding users, redefining the real problem and shaping solutions that feel intuitive and meaningful. Rooted in design, tech and research, my work is driven by a simple belief - design has the power to transform lives.

    My Skills Are:
  • Design: UI/UX Design, Interaction Design, UX Research, System Design
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe CC, Procreate, Framer, Rive
  • Technical: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C++
  • Creative: Illustration, Photography
